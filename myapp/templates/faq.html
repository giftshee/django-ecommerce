How do I start my Shopify store?

Getting started is easy. Sign up for a Shopify account, choose a theme, add your products, and set up payment and shipping options. Shopify’s guides and tutorials make it simple to launch your store quickly.

Can I customize my store design?

Yes! Shopify allows you to customize your store’s appearance using themes and the drag-and-drop editor. You can also add custom code for advanced design changes.

Which payment methods can I accept?

Shopify supports a wide range of payment options including credit cards, PayPal, Shopify Payments, and many third-party gateways. You can enable multiple options to make it easy for customers to pay.

How do I manage shipping and orders?

Shopify provides tools to set up shipping rates, print labels, and track orders. You can automate shipping rules and notifications to keep your customers updated.

Can I sell internationally?

Absolutely! Shopify supports multiple currencies and languages, allowing you to reach customers around the world. You can also manage international shipping and taxes easily.

Can't find the answer to your question?

If you need help with your Shopify store, our support team is ready to assist. From store setup to sales strategies, we provide guidance to help your online shop succeed.
